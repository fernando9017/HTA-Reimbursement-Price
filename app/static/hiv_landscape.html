Overview

This module provides a country-by-country overview of the HIV treatment landscape across markets, focused on access, pricing, and reimbursement for Truvada (emtricitabine/tenofovir disoproxil fumarate) and its generic equivalents. Each country profile covers regulatory registration, procurement pricing, public health programmes, PrEP availability, and the transition to newer DTG-based regimens.
